<p><p>Motor protection market consists of various types of products designed to protect motors from electrical faults and mechanical failures. Overload relays are used to detect excessive current flowing through the motor and cut off power supply to prevent damage. Vacuum contactors provide a reliable option for switching motors on and off. Combination starters integrate overload relays and contactors into a single unit for convenient motor control. Motor protection circuit breakers combine the functions of overload relays and circuit breakers, providing both short-circuit and overload protection in a compact device.</p></p>
