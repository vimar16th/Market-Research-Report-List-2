<p><p>The semiconductor high-performance ceramics market comprises various types of ceramics such as semiconductor aluminas, aluminum nitride, silicon carbide, and silicon nitride. Semiconductor aluminas (Al2O3) ceramics possess excellent thermal properties and are widely used in electronic applications. Semiconductor aluminum nitride (AlN) ceramics exhibit high thermal conductivity and electrical insulation properties, making them suitable for semiconductors. Semiconductor silicon carbide (SiC) ceramics offer high-temperature resistance and excellent mechanical strength. Semiconductor silicon nitride (Si3N4) ceramics are known for their high strength, low density, and good thermal shock resistance, making them ideal for high-performance semiconductors.</p></p>
