<p><p>Manual-tie balers are heavy-duty machines designed to compress and bind various materials for easier storage and transportation. They are ideal for handling materials like cardboard, plastic bottles, and shrink wrap. </p><p>On the other hand, horizontal type balers are larger and more suitable for high-volume operations. They are typically used for compressing bulkier materials such as foam, textile waste, and scrap metal. Both types of manual-tie balers offer efficient waste management solutions but cater to different requirements based on volume and material type.</p></p>
