<p><p>The Thermocouple Temperature Sensor market consists of two types: Contact Thermocouple Temperature Sensor and Non-contact Thermocouple Temperature Sensor. Contact Thermocouple Temperature Sensors are designed to measure temperature by direct contact with the object being measured. They use two different metals connected at the tip, creating an electrical current that varies with temperature. On the other hand, Non-contact Thermocouple Temperature Sensors use infrared technology to measure temperature without physical contact. They measure the electromagnetic radiation emitted by the object and convert it into temperature readings.</p></p>
