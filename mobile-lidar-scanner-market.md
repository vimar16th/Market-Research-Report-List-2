<p><p>The Mobile LiDAR Scanner market can be divided into two main types: Mechanical and Solid-state. Mechanical scanners use rotating mirrors or prisms to steer the laser beam and collect data. These scanners are known for their high accuracy and long-range capabilities, making them suitable for large-scale projects. On the other hand, solid-state scanners utilize small and compact lasers that do not require any moving parts. These scanners are more efficient, reliable, and cost-effective, making them ideal for smaller-scale applications where portability is crucial.</p></p>
