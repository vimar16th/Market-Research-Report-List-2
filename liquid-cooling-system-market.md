<p><p>The Liquid Cooling System market is classified into three types based on the number of radiators used. Triple Radiator Liquid Cooling System incorporates three radiators to dissipate heat efficiently. Dual Radiator Liquid Cooling System uses two radiators to cool down the system. These systems are designed to enhance cooling performance and are suitable for different levels of cooling requirements, ranging from high-end gaming PCs to industrial applications.</p></p>
