<p><p>The screw thread gauges market primarily consists of three types: ring gauge, plug gauge, and others. Ring gauges are used to measure the external diameter of screw threads. They have a cylindrical shape with a threaded hole to check if the screw thread fits perfectly. Plug gauges, on the other hand, are used to measure the internal diameter of screw threads. They have a cylindrical shape with a threaded pin to check if the screw thread fits perfectly. The "others" category includes different types of specialized gauges used for specific screw thread measurements, depending on the industry requirements.</p></p>
