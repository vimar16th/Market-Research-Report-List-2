<p><p>Machine condition monitoring equipment is used to assess the health of machines and anticipate potential failures. Ultrasound detectors use sound waves to detect abnormalities, vibration detectors measure machine vibrations to identify faults, and spectrometers analyze the chemical composition of machine fluids. Infrared sensors detect anomalies in temperature, corrosion probes assess the corrosion levels in machines, and spectral analyzers analyze the spectral signature of machine components. These diverse types of equipment cater to various monitoring needs and assist in predicting and preventing machine failures.</p></p>
