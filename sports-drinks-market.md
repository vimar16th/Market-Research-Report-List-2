<p><strong>The Sports Drinks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isotonic Sport Drink</li><li>Hypertonic Sport Drink</li><li>Hypotonic Sport Drink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sports drinks come in different types based on their composition and purpose. Isotonic sports drinks have a similar concentration of dissolved substances as the body's fluids, making them quickly absorbed and replenishing lost fluids during exercise. Hypertonic sports drinks contain a higher concentration of dissolved substances than the body's fluids and are designed to provide energy and fuel for longer endurance activities. Hypotonic sports drinks have a lower concentration of dissolved substances than the body's fluids and are ideal for rapid rehydration without adding significant energy. These different types cater to varying needs of athletes during different stages of physical activity.</p></p>
