<p><p>Industrial cameras are used in various industries for capturing images and videos. There are two main types of industrial cameras: area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture an entire image at once and are commonly used for quality control and inspection purposes. They are ideal for capturing still images or videos of stationary objects. On the other hand, line scan cameras capture images in a linear fashion, suitable for capturing images of objects in motion, such as moving production lines. This enables high-speed image capture and is often used in applications like printing and packaging.</p></p>
