<p><p>Marking devices, such as laser marking devices and inkjet marking devices, are used to apply identification or labeling information on different materials. Both types of devices have their own advantages and applications. Laser marking devices offer high precision and durability, making them suitable for materials like metal and plastic. Inkjet marking devices, on the other hand, offer versatility and are commonly used for printing on various products, including packaging and labels.</p></p>
