<p><p>Tower-type and rotor-type are both market types in the Industrial and Commercial Desiccant Dehumidifier industry. Tower-type dehumidifiers are designed with a vertical structure and use desiccant materials to remove moisture from the air. They are typically used in large-scale applications, such as industrial plants or commercial buildings. On the other hand, rotor-type dehumidifiers have a horizontal structure and use a rotating desiccant wheel to perform dehumidification. They are commonly used in smaller commercial spaces, such as offices or laboratories. Both types of dehumidifiers serve the purpose of reducing humidity levels and improving air quality in various industrial and commercial settings.</p></p>
