<p><p>DC centrifugal fans refer to fans that are powered by a DC motor. These fans are commonly categorized into three types: forward-curved, backward-curved, and straight radial. </p><p>Forward-curved fans have blades that curve in the direction of rotation, allowing for high airflow but low pressure generation. Backward-curved fans have blades that curve against the rotation direction, providing high pressure generation but limited airflow. Straight radial fans have blades perpendicular to the rotation axis, resulting in a balance between airflow and pressure.</p><p>Each type caters to specific applications and requirements, making them suitable for various industries.</p></p>
