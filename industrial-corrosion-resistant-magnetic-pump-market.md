<p><p>The Industrial Corrosion-resistant Magnetic Pump Market includes two main types: Stationary Shaft and Rotating Shaft. </p><p>In Stationary Shaft pumps, the impeller is attached to a fixed shaft, while the magnet assembly remains stationary, creating a magnetic coupling with the drive magnet. This design allows for easy maintenance and prevents leakage, making it highly suitable for corrosive environments.</p><p>On the other hand, Rotating Shaft pumps have a rotating impeller shaft that is directly connected to the magnet assembly. This configuration provides higher pumping efficiency but requires proper alignment and maintenance to prevent leakage.</p></p>
