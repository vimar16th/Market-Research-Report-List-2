<p><ul><li>Differential Pressure Tubing Anchor</li><li>Pressure Tubing Anchor</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The hydraulic tubing anchor market consists of two main types: the differential pressure tubing anchor and the pressure tubing anchor. </p><p>A differential pressure tubing anchor is designed to hold the tubing string in place by utilizing the pressure difference between the casing and tubing. It features a set of slips that grasp the tubing when the pressure is applied, preventing any vertical movement or slippage.</p><p>On the other hand, a pressure tubing anchor is specifically used to resist tubing movement caused by internal pressure. It functions by gripping the tubing string tightly, ensuring it remains fixed in place and preventing any unwanted shifting or displacement.</p></p>
